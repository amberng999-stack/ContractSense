The Problem

Small and medium businesses (SMEs) in Malaysia face serious challenges when reviewing contracts — from hiring offers to rental and sales agreements. Without affordable legal support, most SMEs sign contracts blind, exposing themselves to financial and legal risks.

High Legal Fees

Hiring a lawyer to review a simple contract costs RM500–RM2,500. Most SMEs cannot afford this and end up signing unchecked contracts, risking serious legal exposure.

Complex Laws & Slow Turnaround

Laws like the Employment Act 1955 and PDPA 2010 are frequently updated. External lawyers take 3–7 days to review contracts, slowing down deals.

Our Solution

ContractSense is an AI-powered contract analysis platform built for Malaysian SMEs. Upload any contract and get instant, affordable legal insights — fully aligned with local laws like the Employment Act 1955 and PDPA 2010 — in seconds, not days.

Instant AI Analysis

Our AI engine scans contracts in seconds, flagging risky clauses, missing terms, and legal inconsistencies, with auto edit and export options immediately.

Malaysian Law Ready

ContractSense is trained on Malaysian legal frameworks including the Employment Act 1955 (2022 amendments) and PDPA 2010. SMEs get compliance checks at a fraction of the cost.
